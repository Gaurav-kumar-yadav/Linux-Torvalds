Biography

Linux Torvalds is a finnish-American software engineer who is the creator and principal developer of the Linux kernal,which is the kernal for the number of the operation system.He is also created a version control system Git.He initially developed Linux as a hobby while studying computer science at the University of Helsinki. Torvalds released the first version of the Linux kernel in 1991 and continued to oversee its development for many years
